Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Gopexly Pro?

Click the “👑 Upgrade” button in the top navigation bar. You can choose monthly or annual billing and pay securely via Paystack.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes. Cancel from Profile → Settings → Subscription. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

Is my portfolio value visible to other users?

Never. Your portfolio naira values are completely private. Only your portfolio percentage return can be shared, and only when you actively choose to share it.

Does Gopexly give investment advice?

No. Gopexly is a financial information and social investing platform, not a licensed stockbroker or investment advisor. All content is for informational purposes only.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently deletes all your data and cannot be undone.

How accurate are the NGX stock prices?

Prices are updated every 10 minutes during Nigerian trading hours from the Nigerian Exchange Group (NGX). They should not be used for time-sensitive trading decisions.
