Privacy Policy
Effective: 1 January 2026  ·  Last updated: May 2026  ·  Gopexly Technologies Ltd  ·  Nigeria

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Gopexly Technologies Ltd (“Gopexly”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use the Gopexly platform at gopexly.com and all associated services.

By creating a Gopexly account you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the platform.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account data: Your name, email address, username, phone number, and password (hashed — never stored in plain text)
  • Profile data: Bio, location, country, profile settings
  • Portfolio data: Stock holdings, buy prices, number of shares, and wealth goals you enter
  • Content: Posts, comments, reactions, direct messages, and course progress
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Paystack. Gopexly stores only your subscription status and plan type — we never store your card details

Information collected automatically

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of access
  • Device information: device type, operating system
  • Usage patterns: features used, posts created, courses completed
  • Session tokens used to keep you logged in securely

Information from third parties

  • Market data from the Nigerian Exchange Group (NGX) for stock prices
  • Payment confirmation from Paystack after successful transactions

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Operating the Gopexly platform and displaying your portfolio
  • Verifying your identity and keeping your account secure
  • Personalising your feed and investment recommendations
  • Sending account verification emails and important platform notifications
  • Processing Pro subscription payments through Paystack
  • Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and enforcing community guidelines
  • Understanding how the platform is used so we can improve it
  • Meeting our legal obligations under Nigerian law including the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019

We will never sell your data, use it for unsolicited marketing, or share it with third parties for their own commercial purposes.

4. Portfolio Privacy — Our Core Commitment

Your portfolio naira (₦) values are completely private. No other user, and no Gopexly administrator, can see the monetary value of your portfolio. This is a fundamental design principle of Gopexly, not just a setting.

  • Visible to others (only if you opt in): Your portfolio percentage return, e.g. “+12.5%”
  • Always private: Your total portfolio value in naira, individual stock values, exact number of shares, and buy prices
  • Always private: Your savings goal amounts, banking information, and payment history

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only in these limited circumstances:

Service providers

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider
  • Paystack: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions
  • Render.com: Our hosting provider
  • Groq: Powers our Gopex AI assistant. Your questions are sent to their API. They do not retain conversation history.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your data if required by Nigerian law, court order, or government authority. We will notify you where legally permitted to do so.

Business transfers

If Gopexly is acquired or merges with another company your data may be transferred. We will notify users before any such transfer occurs.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Gopexly uses the following types of cookies and local storage:

  • Authentication cookies: Required to keep you logged in. These are secure, HTTP-only cookies managed by Supabase.
  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Preference storage: We store in-app preferences in browser local storage.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics cookies that track you across other websites.

7. Data Security

  • All data transmitted uses HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Your data is stored in encrypted Supabase databases
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords
  • Row-Level Security policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Short-lived JWT session tokens with automatic refresh
  • Strict admin-only access to sensitive operations

While we take security seriously, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your Gopexly account.

8. Your Rights Under NDPR

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to data portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to how we process your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time

You can also delete your account directly from Profile → Settings → Delete Account, which permanently erases all your data.

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: All personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Financial records: Subscription payment records retained for 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations
  • Backups: Automated database backups deleted within 90 days
  • Log files: Server access logs retained for 90 days for security purposes

10. Children's Privacy

Gopexly is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes we will post the updated policy on this page with a new effective date and notify all registered users via the in-app notifications system.

Continued use of Gopexly after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
